# Project Proposal Chapter 1 Completion Checklist
This list covers all required sections up to and including Chapter One.

---

## A. Preliminary Pages (Before Chapter 1)
These pages are the front matter and should use Roman numerals (ii, iii, iv, etc.).

### Content & Data
* **Title Page:** Fill in the final Topic, Name, Matric Number, and Defence Month/Year.
* **Certification (Page ii):** Get the Supervisor's and HOD's signatures and dates.
* **Acknowledgements (Page iii):** Write your thank-you message (keep it to two paragraphs max).
* **Abbreviations (Page viii):** Make sure all acronyms used in the project are listed and defined.
* **Abstract:** Write the full, concise summary of the entire project (aim, method, and conclusion).

### Formatting & Consistency
* **Table of Contents (Page iv):** Manually format and update all page numbers to match the final document.
* **List of Figures (Page vi):** Manually format and update all figure titles and page numbers.
* **List of Tables (Page vii):** Manually format and update all table titles and page numbers.
* **General Format:** Check that all preliminary pages use **1.15 line spacing**.

---

## B. Chapter One: Introduction
This chapter starts on Page 1 and uses standard numbering.

### Content

* **1.4. Methodology:** Write a detailed breakdown of the steps and procedures you will use to achieve each objective.
* **Table 1.1: Objectives-Methodology Mapping Table:** Fill in the table completely, matching objectives to their specific methods.
